DUBAI METRO GUIDE

How to Use the Metro?

Using the Dubai Metro system is very easy. Purchase a ticket from the station or use your Nol card. Follow the metro map or ask station officials which line (Red or Green) you need.

What are Nol Cards?

Nol card is a rechargeable electronic ticketing card used to pay fares on metro, tram, and buses.
